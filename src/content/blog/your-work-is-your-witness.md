A word of encouragement for you today — may this encourage your heart.

Excellence isn't a standard the world set. It's a standard God set. It shows up in how you lead every single day. Consistency. Integrity. Quality. Not for applause, for a higher purpose.

> "Whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord and not for men." — Colossians 3:23 (ESV)

Your work is your witness.

Where are you being called to a higher standard in the marketplace and your life?
