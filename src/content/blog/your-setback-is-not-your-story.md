Every leader stumbles. Your setback today is not your story tomorrow.

> "When you stumble today, pick yourself up tomorrow." — John Mason, *Momentum Builders*

In the marketplace and in life, failure is not the finish line. It's part of the journey. The marketplace needs leaders who are resilient, honest, and willing to keep moving forward despite stumbling.

Get back up. Adjust. Keep leading.
