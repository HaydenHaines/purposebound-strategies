What you compromise in private will eventually show up in your leadership, in your team culture, and your business decisions.

King David was a strong leader, but private decisions carried public consequences. Spiritual leadership means guarding your integrity when no one is watching. Your business, your team, and your calling depend on it.

Where do you need to guard your integrity today?
