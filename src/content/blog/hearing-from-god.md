I recently attended the Kingdom at Work Kingdom Leadership Workshop. One breakout session that deeply impacted me was "Hearing From God," led by Casey Brewer and his wife, Dana. They knocked it out of the park!

A few key takeaways to reflect on:

- God's voice brings peace, clarity, and comfort.
- The world's voice brings shame, chaos, and confusion.

True spiritual leadership means learning to lead from His presence, not the noise.
